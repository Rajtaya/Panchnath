About Us

Established 1984 · Chandigarh

Panchnad Shodh Sansthan (Panchnad Research Institute) was established in 1984 with Justice T. U. Mehta, former Chief Justice of Himachal Pradesh High Court, as its first Chairman. It is a leading forum in the Northwest comprising Punjab, Haryana, Himachal Pradesh, Jammu, Chandigarh and Delhi for free exchange of views.

An honest and tireless pursuit of truth in all its manifestations and all fields of social and national life has been the most valued cultural tradition of Bharat. The institute continues this tradition through research, seminars, field studies, interfaith dialogues, and publications.

Guiding Philosophy

"आ नो भद्राः क्रतवो यन्तु विश्वतः"

"एकं सद्विप्रा बहुधा वदन्ति"

Truth is one; the wise express it in many ways
